Here's a comparison table for IRA, 401(k), 403(b), pension, and annuity, covering their names, features, pros, cons, and legal support:

IRA (Individual Retirement Account) A personal retirement savings account with tax advantages; options include Traditional (pre-tax) or Roth (post-tax). Contribution limits for 2025: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+). - Tax-deferred (Traditional) or tax-free (Roth) growth.
