1. Access SimilarChart.com

2. Define the Target Pattern

  • Analyze your uploaded chart:

    • Specify if the sales trend aligns (e.g., stable, growing, or volatile).

3. Select Market & Sector

  • Choose NYSE or NASDAQ under the market filter.
  • Narrow your search to a specific sector if applicable (e.g., technology, healthcare).

4. Search for EPS Patterns

  • Use filters to focus on quarterly EPS trends:
    • Negative or declining EPS for earlier quarters.
    • Gradual improvement or recovery in recent quarters.
  • Specify your time frame (e.g., last 2-3 years).

5. Add Sales Filters

  • Add quarterly sales growth data to your criteria:
    • Set thresholds (e.g., 0%–10% growth, consistent sales performance).
    • Use the “Revenue Trends” feature to prioritize companies showing stable or rising sales.

6. Customize the Time Period

  • Adjust the search range to match your chart’s timeframe:
    • Example: Quarterly data for 2022 Q4–2024 Q3.

7. Run the Search

  • Launch the search with your defined filters and wait for the results.
  • Review the initial matches for similar trends.

8. Analyze the Results

  • Examine matched companies:
    • Review their EPS and sales data visually.
    • Confirm whether the pattern aligns with your chart’s progression.

9. Refine the Results

  • Fine-tune filters for better matches:
    • Adjust EPS growth rate, sales stability, or sector relevance.
    • Add/remove criteria if necessary.

10. Save or Export Findings

  • Save your search results for reference or export the data (if supported).
