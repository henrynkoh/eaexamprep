오늘의 단상_기도에 대한 하나님말씀

성경말씀

2024-10-16 07:34:20

 

오늘은 기도에 대한 하나님말씀이라는 주제로 관련 성경구절들을 살펴보려고 합니다. 일상생활을 통해서 기도에 대한 생각과 기대가 사람마다 크게 차이가 있다는 것을 알게됩니다. 그렇다면 나의 개인적인 지식과 경험에 집착하고 말고 하나님이 어떻게 말씀하셨는지 알아보고 하나님뜻에 따른 바른 기도생활을 하는 것이 중요하다고 생각합니다. 이 주제가 하나님을 아는 지식에서 더욱 성장하는 계기를 제공할 수 있기를 소망합니다.

 

< 주요내용 요약 >

 

  1. 순종과 희생: 하나님은 제물보다 순종을 원하십니다. (사무엘상 15:22)
  2. 진정한 제물: 하나님은 상한 마음을 원하십니다. (시편 51:16-17)
  3. 정의와 공의: 의로운 행동이 제사보다 중요합니다. (잠언 21:3)
  4. 형식적인 예배의 문제: 진실하지 않은 예배는 하나님께 가증합니다. (이사야 66:3)
  5. 자비의 중요성: 하나님은 자비를 원하십니다. (호세아 6:6)
  6. 진실한 예배: 형식적인 예배는 무의미합니다. (예레미야 6:20)
  7. 하나님과 동행: 하나님의 말씀에 순종하는 것이 중요합니다. (예레미야 7:23)
  8. 회개의 부르심: 예수님은 죄인을 부르러 오셨습니다. (마태복음 9:13)
  9. 자비의 이해: 하나님의 자비를 이해하는 것이 중요합니다. (마태복음 12:7)
  10. 사랑의 중요성: 하나님과 이웃을 사랑하는 것이 가장 큰 계명입니다. (마가복음 12:33)

 

이 성경구절들은 우리에게 하나님의 뜻과 우리의 삶의 방향에 대해 깊은 통찰을 제공합니다. 형식적인 종교 행위보다는 진실한 마음과 순종, 자비와 사랑이 하나님께 더 중요하다는 것을 알 수 있습니다.

 

Obedience Over Sacrifice

  1. "And Samuel said, Hath the LORD as great delight in burnt offerings and sacrifices, as in obeying the voice of the LORD? Behold, to obey is better than sacrifice, and to hearken than the fat of rams." (1 Samuel 15:22)
  2. "For I know that it is not the sacrifice of the wicked that pleases God, but obedience to His commands." (Proverbs 21:27)
  3. "To obey is better than sacrifice; it is the heart that God desires." (Micah 6:8)
  4. "If ye love me, keep my commandments." (John 14:15)

 

The True Sacrifice: A Contrite Heart

  1. "For thou desirest not sacrifice; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt offering. The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit: a broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise." (Psalm 51:16-17)
  2. "The LORD is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart; and saveth such as be of a contrite spirit." (Psalm 34:18)
  3. "Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me." (Psalm 51:10)
  4. "He that hath clean hands, and a pure heart; who hath not lifted up his soul unto vanity, nor sworn deceitfully." (Psalm 24:4)

 

Justice and Righteousness Above Sacrifice

  1. "To do justice and judgment is more acceptable to the LORD than sacrifice." (Proverbs 21:3)
  2. "He hath shewed thee, O man, what is good; and what doth the LORD require of thee, but to do justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with thy God?" (Micah 6:8)
  3. "The righteous considereth the cause of the poor: but the wicked regardeth not to know it." (Proverbs 29:7)
  4. "He that followeth after righteousness and mercy findeth life, righteousness, and honour." (Proverbs 21:21)

 

Empty Rituals Are Detestable to God

  1. "He that killeth an ox is as if he slew a man; he that sacrificeth a lamb, as if he cut off a dog's neck; he that offereth an oblation, as if he offered swine's blood; he that burneth incense, as if he blessed an idol. Yea, they have chosen their own ways, and their soul delighteth in their abominations." (Isaiah 66:3)
  2. "Bring no more vain oblations; incense is an abomination unto me; the new moons and sabbaths, the calling of assemblies, I cannot away with; it is iniquity, even the solemn meeting." (Isaiah 1:13)
  3. "What purpose is the multitude of your sacrifices unto me? saith the LORD: I am full of the burnt offerings of rams, and the fat of fed beasts; and I delight not in the blood of bullocks, or of lambs, or of he goats." (Isaiah 1:11)
  4. "Ye offer polluted bread upon mine altar; and ye say, Wherein have we polluted thee? In that ye say, The table of the LORD is contemptible." (Malachi 1:7)

 

Mercy Over Rituals

  1. "For I desired mercy, and not sacrifice; and the knowledge of God more than burnt offerings." (Hosea 6:6)
  2. "Blessed are the merciful: for they shall obtain mercy." (Matthew 5:7)
  3. "But go ye and learn what that meaneth, I will have mercy, and not sacrifice..." (Matthew 9:13)
  4. "The LORD is merciful and gracious, slow to anger, and plenteous in mercy." (Psalm 103:8)

 

Rejecting Insincere Worship

  1. "To what purpose cometh there to me incense from Sheba, and the sweet cane from a far country? your burnt offerings are not acceptable, nor your sacrifices sweet unto me." (Jeremiah 6:20)
  2. "And when ye come to appear before me, who hath required this at your hand, to tread my courts?" (Isaiah 1:12)
  3. "This people draweth nigh unto me with their mouth, and honoureth me with their lips; but their heart is far from me." (Matthew 15:8)
  4. "And why call ye me, Lord, Lord, and do not the things which I say?" (Luke 6:46)

 

Call to Obey and Walk with God

  1. "But this thing commanded I them, saying, Obey my voice, and I will be your God, and ye shall be my people..." (Jeremiah 7:23)
  2. "If ye be willing and obedient, ye shall eat the good of the land." (Isaiah 1:19)
  3. "And thou shalt love the LORD thy God with all thine heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy might." (Deuteronomy 6:5)
  4. "For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works..." (Ephesians 2:10)

 

Mercy and Repentance: The Heart of God's Call

  1. "But go ye and learn what that meaneth, I will have mercy, and not sacrifice..." (Matthew 9:13)
  2. "And Jesus answering said unto them...I came not to call the righteous but sinners to repentance." (Luke 5:32)
  3. "Repent ye therefore, and be converted..." (Acts 3:19)
  4. "The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit..." (Psalm 51:17)

 

Understanding God's Desire for Mercy

  1. "But if ye had known what this meaneth, I will have mercy, and not sacrifice..." (Matthew 12:7)
  2. "For he shall have judgment without mercy that hath shewed no mercy..." (James 2:13)
  3. "The LORD taketh pleasure in them that fear him...in those that hope in his mercy." (Psalm 147:11)
  4. "For we ourselves also were sometimes foolish...but after that the kindness and love of God our Saviour toward man appeared..." (Titus 3:3-4)

 

Love Over Sacrifice

  1. "And to love him with all the heart...is more than all whole burnt offerings and sacrifices." (Mark 12:33)
  2. “Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.” (Matthew 22:39)
  3. “For all the law is fulfilled in one word...Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.” (Galatians 5:14)
  4. “Beloved, let us love one another...for love is of God.” (1 John 4:7)

위에 검토한 성경구절들에는 살아계신 하나님이 피조물인 우리에게 가르치기 원하시는 내용이 충분히 담겨있다고 생각합니다. 근거없이 맹신하는 잘못된 기도행위가 있는지 살펴보고 헛된 수고나 오류에 빠지지 않도록 경계해야 합니다. 하나님께서는 형식적인 종교 행위보다 진실한 마음과 순종, 자비와 사랑을 더 중요시 한다는 것을 바르게 알아야 합니다.
