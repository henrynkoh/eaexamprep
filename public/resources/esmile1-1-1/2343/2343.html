For a W-2 holder, tax tables determine tax liability based on taxable income and filing status, while schedules provide additional details or calculations (e.g., income sources, itemized deductions). This table prioritizes items relevant to W-2 employees, including common schedules for supplemental income or deductions, and excludes highly specialized forms unless broadly applicable. Tax tables and some schedules are embedded in IRS instructions (e.g., Form 1040 instructions), while others are standalone forms.
