Below is a table listing the top 40 items belonging to deductions from Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) when filing a 2024 tax return with a W-2, formatted as requested with columns for Number, Name, Description, Keywords, Requirements, Deadline, Forms, and Legal Support. These are "below-the-line" deductions, meaning they are subtracted from AGI to calculate taxable income. For a W-2 holder filing in 2025 (as of March 10, 2025), this table includes both standard deductions and itemized deductions available under U.S. tax law for the 2024 tax year, as well as certain credits or adjustments that effectively reduce taxable income post-AGI.
Deductions from AGI are claimed on Form 1040, either via the standard deduction (Schedule 1, Line 12a) or itemized deductions (Schedule A), and are governed by the Internal Revenue Code (IRC). Post-TCJA (Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017), many deductions were limited or repealed (e.g., unreimbursed employee expenses), so this list prioritizes those still available to W-2 holders, including common itemized deductions and special provisions. Where 2024 figures (e.g., standard deduction amounts) are estimates, they’re based on 2023 values adjusted for inflation per IRS trends.
