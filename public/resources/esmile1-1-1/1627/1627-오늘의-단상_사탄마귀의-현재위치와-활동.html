"For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places"[2].

"And the Lord said unto Satan, Behold, all that he hath is in thy power; only upon himself put not forth thine hand"[1].

"And the devil that deceived them was cast into the lake of fire and brimstone, where the beast and the false prophet are, and shall be tormented day and night for ever and ever"[6].
