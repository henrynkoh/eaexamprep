Summary of the Verses

These 20 verses collectively reflect Jesus’ instructions and entrustments to His disciples, emphasizing love, obedience, evangelism, peace, service, and perseverance. They call for mutual love (John 13:34, 15:12), spreading the Gospel (Matthew 28:19-20, Mark 16:15), living righteously (Matthew 5:16, 6:33), and following His example (John 13:15, Matthew 16:24). Jesus empowers them with peace (John 14:27, 16:33), commissions them as His sent ones (John 17:18, 20:21), and urges humility and service (Mark 10:43-44). Faith, strength, and witness-bearing (Luke 22:32, Acts 1:8) are also key themes, rooted in His authority and love.
